## Dieter Rams (Braun)

**Era:** 1960s-1990s
**Company:** Braun AG (Germany)
**Style:** Functional minimalism

### 10 Principles of Good Design
1. **Innovative** - Not novelty for its own sake
2. **Useful** - Function over form
3. **Aesthetic** - Only well-executed objects are beautiful
4. **Understandable** - Self-explanatory interface
5. **Unobtrusive** - Tools, not decorations
6. **Honest** - No false promises
7. **Long-lasting** - Not fashionable, therefore timeless
8. **Thorough** - Nothing arbitrary
9. **Environmentally friendly** - Minimal resources
10. **As little design as possible** - "Less but better"

### Key Products for RetroCase
| Product | Year | Parametric Features |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| RT 20 Radio | 1961 | Front grille with horizontal slots, offset dial |
| SK 4 Phonosuper | 1956 | Flat profile, transparent lid, centered controls |
| T 1000 Radio | 1963 | Large circular dial, modular appearance |
| TP 1 Portable | 1959 | Compact box, integrated handle |
| ET 66 Calculator | 1987 | Grid button layout, minimal bezels |

### Design DNA for Parametric Implementation
- **Corner radii:** Subtle, consistent (8-12mm typical)
- **Colors:** White, grey, black, occasional orange accent
- **Materials feel:** Matte plastic, brushed aluminum, wood veneer
- **Grilles:** Horizontal slots, evenly spaced
- **Controls:** Grouped logically, often offset from center

## Mario Bellini (Olivetti)

**Era:** 1960s-1980s
**Company:** Olivetti (Italy)
**Style:** Sculptural, organic wedge forms

### Design Philosophy
- Form follows emotion as much as function
- Objects should have personality
- Soft edges, continuous surfaces
- Integration of interface into form

### Key Products for RetroCase
| Product | Year | Parametric Features |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| Divisumma 18 | 1973 | Wedge profile, rubber membrane keyboard |
| Logos 55 | 1960s | Dramatic wedge, paper roll slot |
| Logos 75B | 1977 | Tilted display, ergonomic angle |
| TCV 250 Terminal | 1966 | CRT integration, angled keyboard |

### Design DNA for Parametric Implementation
- **Profile:** Wedge/prism with 10-25° rake angle
- **Corners:** Large radii, continuous curves
- **Colors:** Warm neutrals, occasional yellow/orange
- **Surfaces:** Continuous, flowing into each other
- **Display angle:** 15-30° tilt toward user

## Jacob Jensen (Bang & Olufsen)

**Era:** 1960s-1990s
**Company:** Bang & Olufsen (Denmark)
**Style:** Sleek Danish minimalism

### Design Philosophy
- Linear, horizontal emphasis
- Thin profiles
- Hidden complexity
- Premium materials

### Key Products for RetroCase
| Product | Year | Parametric Features |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| Beolit 600 | 1970 | Horizontal grille, wood trim |
| Beogram 4000 | 1972 | Flat, tangential tonearm |
| Beosystem 6000 | 1974 | Stacked horizontal modules |

### Design DNA for Parametric Implementation
- **Profile:** Flat, horizontal emphasis
- **Corners:** Sharp to subtle radius
- **Colors:** Black, aluminum, rosewood
- **Grilles:** Dot matrix or horizontal bars
- **Proportions:** Wide and low

## Space Age Designers (Various)

**Era:** 1968-1975
**Companies:** JVC, Weltron, Panasonic, Sanyo
**Style:** Futuristic, spherical, playful

### Design Philosophy
- Inspired by Space Race
- Spherical and pod forms
- Bright colors
- Integrated "all-in-one" design

### Key Products for RetroCase
| Product | Year | Parametric Features |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| JVC Videosphere | 1970 | Helmet/sphere, chain mount, visor opening |
| Weltron 2001 | 1970 | Ball with opening, integrated 8-track |
| Panasonic Panapet R-70 | 1970s | Small ball, chain, bright colors |
| Sanyo RP 1711 | 1970s | Spherical, AM radio |

### Design DNA for Parametric Implementation
- **Profile:** Spherical, elliptical, pod
- **Openings:** Visor-style cutouts, circular
- **Colors:** White, orange, yellow, red
- **Mounting:** Chain, pedestal, built-in stand
- **Proportions:** Compact, self-contained

## Gino Valle (Solari Udine)

**Era:** 1960s
**Company:** Solari Udine (Italy)
**Style:** Functional display, minimal frame

### Key Products
| Product | Year | Parametric Features |
|---------|------|---------------------|
| Cifra 3 | 1966 | Split-flap display, thin profile, minimal bezel |
| Cifra 5 | 1970s | Wall mount, larger format |
